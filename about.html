About Mr. Bean

Mr. Bean was born in Florida in 2019. When he was 5-month-old, he moved to Bellevue, Washionton with his family. He loves the weather and environment. Most time, he stays in the house. Occasionally, he goes to the backyard to eat some weeds and chases some birds.

About the owner

J is Mr. Bean's owner. She was a 10 year-old girl when she got Mr.Bean as a birthday gift. She loves him very much and takes care Mr. Bean everyday.

Everyone in this family loves Mr. Bean. The neighbor also loves him too. He always sit at the front window and look outside through the screens. Sometimes, he will go to the park with us. At beginning, he is afraid to sit in a car. Now, he feels more comfortable and relax.
